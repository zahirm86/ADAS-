YOLO

"You Only Look Once" It is a popular object detection algorithm in computer vision. It processes the entire image in one go, predicting bounding boxes and class probabilities for multiple objects simultaneously, making it efficient for real-time applications like image and video processing.

Obstacle Detection

Obstacle detection in Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) involves using sensors like cameras and radar to identify and assess potential obstacles in the vehicle's path. This crucial feature enhances safety by providing timely warnings or triggering automated actions to avoid collisions.

Need of ADAS

An ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance System) is essential for enhancing road safety and reducing accidents. These systems leverage technology to assist drivers in various ways, such as collision avoidance, lane-keeping, and adaptive cruise control. By providing real-time feedback and assistance, ADAS helps mitigate human errors, improves overall driving experience, and contributes to a safer and more efficient transportation ecosystem.
